About This Prototype

ReactRush is a micro-game built as a rapid ideation prototype to explore interaction design, timing-based logic, and simple performance feedback loops. It uses random delays, time measurement via JavaScript, animation states, and local browser storage to track the best reaction time and recent attempts.
